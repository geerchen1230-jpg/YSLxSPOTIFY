YSL × Spotify
AI-Powered Customer Journey

Instead of blasting one campaign to everyone, this experience follows a single person — Noor — across her full journey with the brand. At each stage, AI reads signals from her Spotify behaviour and triggers a different kind of message.

01 Persona · Who Noor Is

Noor doesn’t chase hype drops. She listens to slow R&B and indie at night, often on repeat while unwinding after work. She uses perfume to feel grounded and confident, especially in intimate or professional settings. For her, a campaign only works if it feels personal, calm, and emotionally resonant — not pushy.

How Spotify “sees” Noor
Tempo: mostly slow · mid energy Skips: low
Listening window: 21:30–01:00 Playlists: “Late night focus”, “Soft R&B”

From these signals, AI recognises Noor as an emotionally driven listener who prefers depth over noise. That becomes the foundation for her entire YSL x Spotify journey.

AI tag: “Noor-type” · emotional, reflective, ritual-oriented

02 Noor’s Customer Journey

Journey stages

Each stage combines three layers: what Noor feels, what the brand sees, and what AI triggers next.

Customer journey · definition

A customer journey is the full path Noor takes with the brand — from first discovery to long-term loyalty. Instead of asking “What do we want to send?”, we ask “Where is Noor in her story, and what does she need from us now?”

  • Awareness: “Who are you?” · Noor just notices the brand.
  • Interest: “This feels relevant.” · She starts exploring.
  • Consideration: “Could this be for me?” · She compares, hesitates.
  • Decision: “I’m ready.” · She chooses whether to buy.
  • Retention: “This fits my life.” · She builds a long-term habit.

03 AI Triggers · What the System Reads

Behind the scenes, AI turns Noor’s tiny behaviours into signals. The goal is not to stalk her, but to time the emotion: to send the right feeling at the right moment.

Signal 01
Listening behaviour

Slow, late-night tracks played on repeat signal emotional attachment. AI chooses softer language, fewer exclamation marks, and emails scheduled after 21:00 instead of during work hours.

Signal 02
Interaction depth

If Noor scrolls through the Scent Wrapped page and completes a short quiz, AI treats her as “high-intent, low-pressure”, triggering storytelling emails instead of aggressive promotional codes.

Signal 03
Cart & hesitation

Adding a fragrance to cart and leaving signals hesitation. For Noor, AI suggests a small, intimate invitation (sample, early access) instead of shouting “20% OFF!!!”.

Signal 04
Post-purchase engagement

If she opens ritual tips and saves the curated playlist, AI shifts from selling to nurturing: more guidance, more mood-based content, fewer calls to buy again immediately.

Key takeaway

For Noor, this is not an email campaign. It’s a guided emotional journey that starts with her Spotify habits and ends with a stable ritual in her real life. For YSL, this turns “one-time buyer” into “long-term relationship”.

“We’re not sending emails — we’re sending the right emotion at the right moment.”
